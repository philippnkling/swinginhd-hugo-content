This workshop, focused on solo dancing to jazz music, offers a unique blend of classes and practice time that help you see real progress right away.

During the classes, Kris will share her latest understanding of the dance to spark new ideas. Then, in the practice sessions, she’ll provide the time and support you need to incorporate these insights into your dancing and allow you to develop your own unique style.

- [Link to the Workshop](https://swingstep.com/events/boost-your-solo/)
- Address: Kurfürsten-Anlage 58, 69115 Heidelberg
- Cost: 100€, 70€ if you have a regular Full Pass at SwingStep
- Time: November 22 and November 23, 2025, the times are for each day
  * Start: 2:00 p.m.
  * End: 5:30 p.m.
